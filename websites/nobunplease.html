    Subway Keto Menu | 15+ Low Carb Menu Items

    When the mood for a sandwich strikes, pull up this Subway Keto Menu to make ordering so simple!

    With just a few changes to the standard menu, it's easy to find a low carb option without ruining your progress.

    See more: Keto Friendly Fast Food Restaurants

    Overview

    • Almost any sandwich can be made into a suitable option for the Keto diet by requesting a salad instead of bread.
    • Keto is a low carb diet that reduces carbohydrates and focuses primarily on protein and fat. Order double meat to keep these macros high.
    • Avoid the menu options with sweet sauces, such as the Sweet Onion Chicken Teriyaki and Meatball Marinara.
    • Instead of purchasing an entire 12" sub, it's typically more cost effective to just purchase a 6" with double meat.
    • Load up on low carb veggies such as peppers, black olives, lettuce and pickles.
    • Some locations have regional products like roast beef, rotisserie style chicken, and even seafood salad!
    • Take advantage of the Subway app for easy ordering and special offers.
