Cinnamon Swirl Cake Recipe

INGREDIENTS

1 box of Pillsbury™ Cinnamon Swirl Quick Bread

Additional ingredients to prepare cake mix per package instructions

1 tub of Pillsbury™ Creamy Supreme® Cream Cheese Frosting

PREPARATION DIRECTIONS

Step 1

Prepare batter according to the package instructions

Step 2

Prepare two 6” cake pans with parchment paper and baking spray. Fill partway with batter then add a layer of cinnamon sugar from the package. Add more batter to cover. 

Step 3

Bake and cool your cakes (they must be completely cool to frost)

Step 4

Fill a piping bag with cream cheese frosting

Step 5

Layer the cakes with a layer of cream cheese frosting in between and on top. 

Step 6

Sprinkle with cinnamon sugar from package and enjoy!
