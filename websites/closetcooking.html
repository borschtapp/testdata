Jalapeno Popper Skillet Chicken

[heart_this] · Oct 18, 2017 · 12 Comments

Chicken in a cheesy jalapeno sauce! All of the flavours of jalapeno poppers in a skillet chicken dinner!

Today I have a quick, easy and super tasty meal for you, a jalapeno popper skillet chicken aka cheesy jalapeno chicken! This chicken dinner is so easy to make! You simply cook the chicken in a skillet and then make a cheesy jalapeno sauce with cream cheese, cheddar cheese and, of course, jalapenos! These flavours are very similar to jalapeno poppers, or jalapenos stuffed with cream cheese and cheddar cheese so I like to call this a jalapeno popper skillet chicken. Since I sometimes like to wrap my cheese stuffed jalapeno poppers with bacon, it makes a perfect addition to this dish! This recipe comes together in about as much time as it takes to cook some rice and steam some veggies which complete the meal and make it perfect for busy work days!




Top with extra cheese!

Jalapeno Popper Skillet Chicken

Prep Time: 5 minutes Cook Time: 15 minutes Total Time: 20 minutes Servings: 5

Chicken in a cheesy jalapeno sauce! All of the flavours of jalapeno poppers in a skillet chicken dinner!

ingredients
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 pound chicken, boneless and skinless, diced
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 jalapenos, sliced or diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, chopped
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
directions
  1. Heat the oil in a pan over medium-high heat, add the chicken (seasoned with salt and pepper) and cook until lightly golden brown.
  2. Add the onions and jalapenos and cook until tender, about a minute before adding the garlic and cooking another minute.
  3. Add the chicken broth and deglaze the skillet by scraping the brown bits up off of the bottom of the pan as the broth sizzles.
  4. Add the cheese and cook until it has melted and the sauce is nice and smooth
Option: Start by cooking 4 strips bacon until crispy, set aside and use the bacon grease in place of oil before continuing with the recipe and serving with the crumbled bacon!
Nutrition Facts: Calories 415, Fat 32g (Saturated 13g, Trans 0.2g), Cholesterol 117mg, Sodium 361mg, Carbs 4g (Fiber 0.4g, Sugars 2g), Protein 25g
